# The Three Fatigues

Everyone's talking about AI fatigue. But what are we actually tired of?

It's not one thing. It's three. And they feed each other in a paradox that explains why the AI era feels so exhausting despite being so productive.

## AI Fatigue: "There's too much AI"

- New tools every week
- Every product adds an AI button
- Your toolkit changes before you've mastered the last one

**MBS Layer**: Body. The infrastructure is churning.

## Decision Fatigue: "I can't choose"

- Which AI model? Claude? GPT? Gemini?
- Which workflow? Spec-driven? PRD-driven? Vibe coding?
- Should I learn this tool, or will it be obsolete by Tuesday?

**MBS Layer**: Mind. Too many options, not enough frameworks.

## Judgement Fatigue: "I can't tell what's real"

- Is this AI-generated code actually correct?
- Is this AI hype genuine, or the next crypto?
- Am I using AI to think, or am I letting it think for me?

**MBS Layer**: Soul. The existential questions.

## The Paradox

Here's where it gets interesting:

**AI fatigue creates decision fatigue** (too many tools, can't choose). **Decision fatigue creates judgement fatigue** (can't choose, stop trusting your own judgement). **Judgement fatigue creates AI fatigue** (don't trust judgement, offload to AI, AI proliferates, too many tools).

It's a self-reinforcing spiral. And it maps perfectly to MBS:

```
Body overload → Mind overload → Soul crisis → Body overload → ...
```

## The Way Out

The paradox breaks when you address the **Mind layer first**:

1. **One framework, not many tools.** A cognitive architecture gives you verbs that apply to any domain. Stop collecting tools. Start building a system.
2. **Structured evaluation, not vibes.** A deliberation protocol gives you a method for "is this real?" Stop trusting gut feelings about AI. Start testing claims.
3. **Identity clarity, not tool mastery.** A self-model gives you "what is my role in this?" Stop asking which AI. Start asking who you are becoming with AI.

The antidote to fatigue isn't rest. It's clarity.

## ⚛️ The Fusion

> The industrial era gave us physical fatigue. The information era gave us mental fatigue. The AI era gives us **judgement fatigue**: the exhaustion of not knowing whether to trust yourself or the machine.

## What to Do Next

Stop evaluating tools. Start building frameworks.

Stop asking "which AI?" Start asking "what am I trying to think about?"

Stop worrying about keeping up. Start worrying about knowing what matters.
