Mimicry is the safest form of failure.

It feels like progress because you are following a 'proven' playbook, but it is actually a slow death of the soul.

No amount of borrowed identity can substitute for the real thing. Weave your own pattern. For you to weave the beads of identity, you must first untie the knot of mimicry.

Stop asking what the industry is doing. Start asking what only you would bother doing.

Are you running an industry playbook, or weaving your own pattern?
