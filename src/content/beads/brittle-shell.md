It is easy to hold strong values when the market is up and the quarter is smooth.

But identity is not what you claim on a peaceful day. It is what you refuse to discard when everything is on fire.

If a cultural value is the first thing sacrificed for a short-term gain, what you have is not a culture.

It is a convenience. An identity that cannot survive a crisis was never an identity; it was a luxury.

What values will you refuse to sacrifice when it costs money to uphold them?
