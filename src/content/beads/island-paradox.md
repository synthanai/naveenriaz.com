When a team must build walls to protect its efficiency or psychological safety from the rest of the enterprise, they become cultural orphans.

They perform well, but their survival instinct breaks the circulatory system of the organisation.

Do not applaud the fortress. Ask why the surrounding environment made it necessary to build one.

A high-performing silo is usually a symptom of a low-performing ecosystem.

Are your most productive teams structurally isolated from the enterprise?
