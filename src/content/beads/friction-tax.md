Most of the slowness in your organisation is not a talent problem. It is an alignment problem.

When the intent is undeclared or ambiguous, every interaction becomes a negotiation.

People spend half their energy guessing what the leader actually wants.

Declare the intent. The tax disappears. Friction is the debt of undeclared intent.

Is your operational speed slowed down by endless negotiation disguised as process?
