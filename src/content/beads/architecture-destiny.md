We are often told that strong culture alone can scale an organisation.

This is a dangerous myth. Willpower runs out. Motivation fluctuates.

You cannot train your way out of a bad structural design. If the incentives reward hoarding, the culture will hoard, regardless of what the posters in the hallway say about collaboration.

The architecture of your organisation is the only thing that remains awake when everyone else is tired.

Are your values on the wall, or built into the incentive structure?
