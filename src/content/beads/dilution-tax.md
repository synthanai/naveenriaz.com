You cannot commodify the soul of your organisation without bankrupting it.

When purpose is extracted from the centre and painted onto the perimeter purely for public consumption, the internal engine stops running.

A purpose that requires a communication strategy to be believed is already dead.

A living purpose is felt in how decisions are made, not in how they are announced.

Are your employees inspired by your purpose, or just adjusting their engagement to tolerate it?
