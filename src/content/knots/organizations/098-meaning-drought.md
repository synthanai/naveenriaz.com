One who stands free of folly creates millions.
Valluvar values purposeful clarity.
Symptoms: competent work. Diagnosis: zero meaning.
Your team performs but can't say why. They deliver features nobody asked for. They hit targets nobody explained. They complete sprints that feel like loops.
The work gets done. The point gets lost.
Meaning drought isn't about bad work. It's about good work disconnected from purpose. The team is competent and empty.

WD-40 Company has a 93% employee engagement rate.
I investigated how.
CEO Garry Ridge, who led the company for 25 years, built the culture around 'tribal belonging.' Employees don't have managers; they have 'coaches.' Mistakes aren't failures; they're 'learning moments.' The company's purpose, 'creating positive lasting memories in everything we do,' extends internally.
I found the financial correlation: WD-40's stock returned 2,300% during Ridge's tenure.
The anti-example of meaning drought is not a company with a clever mission statement.
It is a company where meaning is the operating system, not the screensaver.

When the water table drops, the surface appears unchanged.
In hydrology, aquifer depletion is invisible from the surface. The grass is still green. The wells still produce water. But the underground source is dropping.
When the aquifer empties, the depletion isn't gradual at the surface. It's sudden: wells that produced water yesterday produce nothing today.
Meaning drought works identically: the team appears productive. Metrics are met. Work is delivered. But the underground source of energy, the purpose that drives discretionary effort, is depleting.
When it empties, the symptoms are sudden: disengagement, resignation, apathy. Not because the work changed. Because the meaning behind it disappeared.

Connect one task to one impact this week.
Not 'this feature serves the Q2 strategy.' That's abstraction.
'This feature helps parents track their child's learning.' That's meaning.
The more specific the impact, the fuller the aquifer.

That empty well has a name. **Meaning Drought**.
And once you see it, you can't unsee it.

## Untie The Knot

**Uproot**

Meaning dried up because purpose was stated once and never connected to daily work. The mission statement existed. The link between the mission and Tuesday's tasks was absent.

**Navigate**

Every team can articulate how their work connects to one real human impact. Not strategy. Impact. Specific and tangible.

**Tool**

CORE / Calling: the diagnostic that connects individual work to organizational purpose. When Calling is felt, not just described, meaning flows.

**Implement**

Connect one task to one specific human impact this week. The more specific the impact, the fuller the aquifer.

**Emerge**

When meaning is specific and connected, engagement deepens, discretionary effort returns, and the team works because it wants to, not because it's paid to.
