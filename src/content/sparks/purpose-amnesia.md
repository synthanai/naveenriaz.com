We started a bakery to feed the hungry. Now we're a logistics company optimizing delivery routes for stale bread. 

When did we lose the smell of the yeast? Companies get so good at "how" that they forget "why." Optimization is great, but optimization without purpose is just a faster race toward a finishing line no one cares about. 

Go back to the kitchen today. Find the smell. 
Purpose is a compass, not a spreadsheet.
