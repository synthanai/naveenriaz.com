If your values are on the wall but not in the hallway, they're just indoor decoration. 

Does a mistake lead to a "learning session" or a "blame session"? Does a dissenting opinion get a seat at the table or a cold shoulder? Your culture isn’t what you say you value; it’s what you actually reward and what you actually tolerate. 

Check your behavior, not your banner. 
Culture is a practice.
