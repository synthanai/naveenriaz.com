AI isn’t just a chatbot that talks back; it’s an engine that needs a harness. 

If your "AI approach" is just a text box, you're missing the product. The harness is the infrastructure—the rules, the memory, the state, the auditing. Without a strong harness, your AI is just a loud guest who forgets the conversation halfway through. 

Stop prompt-tuning. Start harness-building. 
The infrastructure is the product.
