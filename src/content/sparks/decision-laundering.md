Asking for "feedback" when you've already decided is just looking for a witness to the deed. 

It’s called decision laundering: you take a private choice and run it through a public committee to make it look "collaborative." But the team knows. They know their input is a prop. And next time, they won't even bring the prop to the meeting. 

Be honest: do you want their input or their signature? 
Authenticity builds trust. Theater destroys it.
