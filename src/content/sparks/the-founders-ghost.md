"We do it this way because the person who started the company said so." 

But the person who started the company in 1994 doesn't live in the world of 2026. Living in a founder’s shadow is respect; living in a founder’s ghost is stagnation. Every generation of a team needs to reclaim the purpose as their own. 

Honor the past. But lead the future. 
The ghost doesn’t have the keys to your success.
