Don't automate the task; orchestrate the intent. 

If you automate a task, you're just doing a robot's job faster. If you orchestrate the *intent*, you're building a system that can adapt when the task changes. The task is what you do; the intent is *why* you're doing it. 

Automate for efficiency. Orchestrate for evolution. 
The "why" is your competitive advantage.
