In 2005, most employees did not have an email address. By 2010, not having one was unthinkable.

In 2026, most employees do not manage a personal brand. By 2030, not having one will feel the same way.

AI is about to remove every friction that currently makes personal branding optional:
1. **Creation**: Drafts from voice memos in 60 seconds.
2. **Consistency**: Persistent digital twins maintain your presence.
3. **Language**: Real-time adaptive translation across cultures.
4. **Personality**: Introverts get visible through artifacts, not performance.

When the friction disappears, the competitive gap between organisations will not be measured in marketing spend. It will be measured in trust.

Does your organisation have a signal worth amplifying before the amplifier becomes free?
