# The Complacency Trap

Do not be an idiot!

You already know the antidote for complacency!

It is stepping out of your comfort zone.
It is gaining new perspectives on how to operate.
It is watching people solve your exact problems in better ways.

We call this travelling!

But here is the trap:

We often confuse planning the journey 
with actually taking the journey.

Reading a framework is not gaining a new perspective.
Thinking about methodologies is not working differently.

Just thinking about travelling is not travelling!

Every time Fathima Nisha and I have packed up and moved countries,
it breaks the autopilot and puts us on high alert!

If you want the antidote to complacency, 
you have to actually walk the path.

Stop planning; Start travelling!
