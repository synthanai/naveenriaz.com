If your feedback only comes once a year, it's not a conversation; it's an autopsy. 

You're telling someone why they "died" six months ago. High-fidelity teams give feedback like breath: constant, necessary, and integrated into the work. If you wait for the "official" review, you've already lost the game. 

Stop checking the pulse once a year. Start breathing together every day. 
Feedback is fuel.
