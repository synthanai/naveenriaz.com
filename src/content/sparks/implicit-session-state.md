Don't tell the AI who you are every time you talk to it. 

The future of high-fidelity agents is "Implicit Session State"—a memory architecture that survives across conversations without the user needing to "re-prompt" or context-dump. The AI should remember your preferences like a long-term partner, not a stranger with a fish-brain. 

Build for memory. Act for the moment. 
Context should be a foundation, not a chore.
