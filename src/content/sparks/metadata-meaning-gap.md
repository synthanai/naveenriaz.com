A search engine finds documents containing "trust." A knowledge system understands that the trust problem connects to a hiring decision made six months ago and the feedback culture that was never established.

That gap, between finding and understanding, is where most "AI-powered knowledge" falls short.

RAG has become the default enterprise architecture. But most implementations are search engines with a language model on top. They retrieve by keyword similarity. They do not understand relationships between ideas or the context that makes one piece of information relevant and another irrelevant.

We built a system that tracks not just what was written, but why and how it connects to everything else.

Stop building smarter search. Start building understanding.
