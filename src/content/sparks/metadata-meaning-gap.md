Indexers are easy. Understanding is hard. 

Most "RAG" systems are just glorified search engines that find keywords. But keyword matching is not "meaning." To bridge the gap, you need a "Cognitive Thread"—a way for the system to understand *why* a piece of data matters in the larger ecosystem. 

Stop collecting metadata. Start building models of meaning. 
Intelligence is about connections, not just collection.
