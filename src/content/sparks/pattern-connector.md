A factory has a quality issue. Marketing underperforms. The support queue grows. Three teams, three budgets, three consultants.

But the quality issue exists because a process change was made without consulting the floor. Marketing underperforms because the product promise no longer matches reality. Support grows because neither team is talking to the other.

One thread. Three symptoms.

In the AI era, the "how" is increasingly free. AI writes code, drafts reports, generates analysis. What it cannot do is look across the loom and see how disparate threads weave into a pattern.

That is the Pattern Connector: defined not by domain expertise but by cross-domain vision.

Anyone can see the thread. Few can see the weave.
