Your business isn't a mess of a hundred problems. It's a web with one central knot. 

If you pull at the wrong thread, you make the knot tighter. But if you find the *one* knot—the root cause of trust, or identity, or structure—then every other problem starts to unweave itself. 

Stop fixing a hundred threads. Find the one knot. 
Focus is the ultimate lever.
