You already know what you need to do, but you are caught in a loop.

Most people try to solve this with discipline. They add friction to bad behaviors: blocking apps, setting alarms, writing 10-step protocols. 

They are fighting gravity with willpower, and gravity always wins.

Transformation is not about discipline. It is about shifting your internal "thermostat" and building a stronger gravitational field. 

Before the week ends, ask yourself these three reality-check questions:

1. **On Identity:** If you observed your last 30 days from the outside with no access to your thoughts, what would you conclude this person believes they deserve?
2. **On Attention:** What are you building right now that is so compelling you would rather work on it than be distracted by your feed? If the answer is nothing, that is the first problem to solve. 
3. **On Influences:** Whose voice do you hear in your head when you are about to take a risk? And are they qualified to have an opinion on what is possible for you?

You do not believe your way into a new life. You act your way into new beliefs. Trust only movement.
