Potholes in the road don't stop the car, but they destroy the suspension over time. 

Culture gaps are the tax you pay on every single transaction in your business. Every time someone clarifies a "hidden meaning," every time someone waits for "the right vibe" to say something—you're paying the tax. 

Fix the road. Save the suspension. 
High-fidelity cultures have zero friction.
