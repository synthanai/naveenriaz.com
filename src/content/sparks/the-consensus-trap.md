If everyone has to agree, the only thing you'll ever do is the most boring, safe, and mediocre thing possible. 

Consensus is the death of innovation. High-fidelity teams don't look for agreement; they look for the best argument. They "Disagree and Commit." They know that 100% agreement usually means 0% passion. 

Stop looking for consensus. Start looking for clarity. 
Tension is a feature, not a bug.
